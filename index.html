About

The best part about working in a team was having someone to work through problems and rubber duck with. I want to live in a world where puppies are plentiful and the coffee is free. Before I was a Dev, I was a Corporate Trainer and it made me good at looking at problems from someone else’s perspective. When I’m not coding, I love getting out and exploring greenspaces, taking in the latest blockbuster, and planning my next great adventure you can find me at the rink honing my roller skating skills and searching the city for the next great cup of joe. Now that I’m done bootcamp, I’m excited to begin the Javascript30 challenge and continue building with React. I’m also excited to learn about Node, Web Accessibility, and Wordpress.
